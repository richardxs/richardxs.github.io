Fuel Efficiency and Its Impact on the Choice of Automobiles

« 1 2 3 »


Although electric cars outperform their fossil fuel countperparts in fuel efficiency, customers still need to consider other factors including size, easy access to gas stations and financial reasons. Nowadays most car makers manufacture 4-cylinder gasoline cars because they are cheap, spacious and there is no shortage of gas stations. To provide guidance on selecting cars, the following plot compares different brands in regards to their average city and highway MPG.

Scene 3: Both the Average City MPG/Highway MPG plots suggest that Japanese and Korean cars tend to have a higher MPG, suggesting that they are more fuel efficient. But the differences are relatively small between different brands, showing that 4-cylinder gasoline cars are usually fuel efficient;
